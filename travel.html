Travels Abroad

My family has always travled, and such it has become a part of my own self expression. Expirinceing other places and cultures, esspecially though the camera, has been an eye opening expirince and an exercise in appriciation of difference. I have had the fortune to travel all over the world. My favorite place that I have been? Coppenhagen Denmark. Now the intersection of the European Cafe culture blended with a modern-ish city that still has walking districts is amazing. The people are so kind and wonderful to talk to, at least in my expirience. Somehting else that is super cool to me as anamerican is the presence of public transportation all across europe. in denmark particularly there was ahuge bike culture, everyone would be riding around on a little sepoerate road built next to the road for cars to keep the bikers safer. I abslutley love living in and experiencing other cultures around the world.
